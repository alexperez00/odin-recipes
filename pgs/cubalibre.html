Cuba Libre

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce rum
  • 3 ounces Coca-Cola
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 ounces bourbon (or rye whiskey, if preferred)
  • Garnish: lime wedgeCu

Steps

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice, then add rum and Coca-Cola.
  2. Garnish with a lime wedge. Squeeze the lime into your drink, if desired.
